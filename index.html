Out Of Sight

WHAT IS THE GAME ABOUT?

Out Of Sight is an online multiplayer hide and seek game. The game is based around a group of criminals that tried to rob the bank, but the cops arrived. When the game start some people will become the criminals and the other will become the police. The criminals' goal is to try to stay out of sight and stay hidden for as long as possible. The cops' goal is to find all criminals and arrest them!
